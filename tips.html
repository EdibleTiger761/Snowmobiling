Tips For Beginners

Some tips i have for beginners are:

  • Ride With Friends
  • Be Careful
  • Ask for help if you need it
  • Ride on trails
  • Prepare for the ride
  • Make sure you have a full tank of gas
  • Take a snowmobile safety course
