What is a Trail Snowmobile?

A trail snowmobile is a snowmobile designed for groomed trails and are not meant for deeper snow, they typcialy have 120 - 137 inches in track length.

They are faster and are meant for speed and comfortable long rides on groomed trails.

How Is It Diffrent From Other Types Of Snowmobiles?

Trail snowmobiles arent meant for deeper snow and have shorter tracks and stiffer suspension,Their handlebars are lower and are meant for sitting rather then standing.

These are nice for beginners.
